Mutton Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • Mutton
  • Rice
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani masala (or a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Cashews and raisins (optional)
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions:

Marinate mutton pieces in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and chopped mint and coriander leaves. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. In a separate pan, saut√© sliced onions until golden brown, add tomatoes and cook until soft, then add the marinated mutton and partially cook. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer partially cooked basmati rice over the mutton, and sprinkle with mint, coriander, fried onions, and ghee. Seal and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Garnish with fried cashews and raisins, and a drizzle of lemon juice before serving. Fluff the biryani gently with a fork and enjoy the rich and aromatic mutton biryani! Adjust spice levels according to taste.

Nutrients:

Proteins, Carbohydrates, Fats, Vitamins and Minerals
